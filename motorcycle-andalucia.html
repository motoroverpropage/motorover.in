Southern Spain & Portugal Motorcycle Tour 2025

MotoRover has been getting the early Sun in Europe since the year 2019 with our riding season starting with the motorcycle tour of Andalusia in Southern Spain. This year, we have added the Algarve region of Spain’s neighboring country, Portugal, to offer an exciting two-country riding experience on the Iberian Peninsula!

The ride starts from the port city of Malaga in Spain and covers all the major cities in Southern Spain before crossing over into Portugal. A day’s break on the coast of Portugal will let you relax and enjoy the Sun, sand and Sea and then we explore the countryside of Algarve. Our route returns to Spain for the final leg of the journey as we make our way back to Malaga via Seville and the white villages of Spain.

The riding route has a rich mix of coastal rides with spectacular sea views and twisty roads in the Sierras (mountains). You will be passing olive fields and national parks along the way with pit stops for local sightseeing and experiences.

This is an 8-day/7-night tour in March 2025 led by an expert motorcycle guide from MotoRover. We are offering three choices of BMW GS adventure motorcycles with fuel, insurance and a top box for the ride and riders’ luggage will follow in the support van driven by a second guide from MotoRover. Group size will be limited to 12 riders and you have the option of getting a plus one for the pillion seat on their bike. We have beautiful 4-star hotels or similar level boutique properties for the overnight stays and we’ll be serving delicious cuisine during the tour as per your dietary preferences. The inclusion of airport pickup and drop and local sightseeing and experiences makes it an all-inclusive ground package motorcycle tour so you can focus on the holiday and not the planning.

All you have to do is arrive and ride! Kindly go through the details of this tour further in this brochure and contact us to book your spot!
