Balkan Motorcycle Tour 2026

The Balkan region is a hidden treasure for travellers looking for a quintessential East European experience. Blessed with the moderate Mediterranean climate, a long coastline and mountain ranges, the Balkan peninsula has the best of everything. Postcard cities with a medieval charm, beaches with turquoise waters, natural wonders and thrilling roads in the mountains and along the Dalmatian Coast.

Our tour covers three countries- Croatia, Bosnia & Herzegovina and Montenegro which were all part of the erstwhile communist nation of SFRY (Yugoslavia). These are Slavic countries with a common culture and influences from Russia, Germany, Austria, Hungary, Italy and even the Turks who have all had political influence over this region through history.

We are offering you a range of high-end touring motorcycles to explore the three countries and you will thoroughly enjoy the great mix of riding and experiences we have carefully curated.
