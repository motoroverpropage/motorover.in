New Zealand Motorcycle Tour 2024

New Zealand is one of the most beautiful countries in the world and it is best explored by road. It is a land blessed with natural beauty and a great variety of features like mountains, glaciers, rivers, tropical forests, and a long coastline, since it is an island nation.

It is known as the ‘Land Of The Long White Cloud’ due to its shape as seen from the sky and its Māori name (Māori were the first inhabitants of the islands before British colonization) is Aotearoa. It is an isolated archipelago in the South Pacific Ocean with two major landmasses, the North Island and the South Island. The southern island is bigger and more beautiful and that is where we conduct our motorcycle tour of New Zealand.

Although an independent country since the year 1947, New Zealand has a British way of life. The ‘Kiwis’ as they are called, speak English, eat English, drive on the left side of the road and allow riding based on a Indian driving license, which makes it very easy to go around.

Our motorcycle tour of New Zealand is a 9 day/8-night guided road trip starting and ending in the biggest city on the South Island, Christchurch. The tour will be led by a guide on a motorcycle and the participant bikes will be followed by luggage van. Besides the riding, the tour package also takes care of other travel requirements like accommodation, meals, airport transfers and local experiences.

Join us for an epic adventure down under to end the year on a high!
