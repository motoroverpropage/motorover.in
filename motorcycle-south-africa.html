South Africa Motorcycle Tour 2025

It’s time for Africa, again! After a successful ride in April 2025, MotoRover is back with another round of the South Africa Motorcycle Adventure scheduled for late October this year.

This is a 9-day guided motorcycle tour conducted in a fly and ride format. This means all you have to do is book the tour, get your South Africa visa and fly into Cape Town. Every service from the time you arrive in Cape town till your departure is covered in our comprehensive tour package. Our ground team includes an expert motorcycle guide leading the group and a luggage van following our convoy to ensure that you travel with full support and safety. You will be given a rental BMW GS motorcycle as per your choice with a top box and fuel for the entire distance of the tour. Also included are 4 star or similar level hotels, all meals and bottled water, activities and entrance fees to nature reserves as per the tour program and airport pickup and drop in Cape town.

Our riding route starts and ends in the seaside city of Cape Town and traces the famous ‘Garden Route’ of South Africa, the most naturally beautiful region of the country. It includes the spectacular cliffside rides along Chapman’s Peak, visit to the Cape of Good Hope and Cape Point, the southern-most point of the African continent at Cape Agulhas, winding roads, mountain passes and gravel sections.

Along with the ride, you shall be enjoying exciting sights and activities like the Big 5 wildlife Safari, Cango caves in Oudtshoorn, Penguins at Boulder beach, a visit to the wine region of South Africa and a rest day in Knysna with multiple activity options. Entries will be limited to 12 riders and their pillions. So come along and join us on this adventure in the rainbow nation!
