MOTOROVER BOOKING POLICY

  • 25% of the tour price has to be paid to confirm booking.

  • Balance 75% amount of the tour price to be paid on or before 7 September

MOTOROVER CANCELLATION POLICY

  • If a tour booking is cancelled more than 45 days prior to the start date, 25% of the tour price will be charged and balance 75% will be refunded
 
  • If a tour booking is cancelled 45-31 days prior to start date, 75% of the tour price will be charged and balance 25% will be refunded
 
  • If a tour booking is cancelled 30 days or less prior to the start date, 100% of the tour price will be charged. There will be no refund
 
  • If a tour booking is cancelled but we get a replacement for the participant, 100% of the payment will be refunded
 
  • If the tour is cancelled by MotoRover due to unavoidable circumstances, 100% of the tour price will be refunded

MOTOROVER CHILD POLICY

  • Children in the age group 0 to 4 year are not allowed on MotoRover Motorcycle tours
 
  • Children in the age group 5 to 9 years are charged the minimum amount as per the policy of MotoRover service partners
 
  • Parents have to additionally bear the cost of a child safety seat (if applicable) and travel insurance
 
  • Children aged 10 years and above will be charged 100% tour price
 
  • Persons below 18 years of age are not allowed to travel on a motorcycle during our tours. However, children aged five or older can be a part of our motorcycle tours by travelling in the back-up vehicle.
