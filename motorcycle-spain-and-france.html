Spain & France Motorcycle Tour 2025

Motorcycle Tour • 8 days

About the Tour

Spain and France are among the most beautiful countries in western Europe and we are offering you a taste of both on a motorcycling adventure!

Our route explores the border region of the two countries and has a wide variety of scenery as it traces through seaside towns, picturesque alpine passes in France and rocky landscapes of Spain.

This weeklong road trip begins and ends in the Spanish city of Barcelona. Ride world-class motorcycles on fantastic roads along Costa Brava (north-eastern coast of Spain) with panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea. Conquer some of the legendary mountain in the Pyrenees mountains that feature on the Tour de France bicycle race. Visit a motorcycle museum with a great collection of specimens from around the world and a museum of Grand Prix legend Marc Marquez in his hometown. Motorcycle Grand Prix racing enthusiasts will love the optional visit to the Catalan round of MotoGP and we have beautiful properties for overnight stays during the journey.

The group will lead by MotoRover's expert tour guide on a motorcycle along with a tour manager driving the support car. The support car will follow the riders to ensure no one is left behind and will carry your luggage too.

All you have to do is sign up, arrive and ride!
