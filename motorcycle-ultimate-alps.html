Ultimate Alps Motorcycle Tour 2026

The Alps Mountain range of Europe needs no introduction. It is the most naturally beautiful region of the continent, famous for its snow-covered peaks, spectacular alpine landscapes, ski resorts and twisty mountain passes.

MotoRover’s first encounter with the Alps was in 2019 where we, along with BMW Motorrad India, organized the biggest international motorcycle tour ever done by an Indian group. The numbers? 43 riders and 11 pillions!

We are reviving this epic motorcycle adventure and it’s going to be even better in 2026. The tour includes the most legendary mountain passes in the Alps, and the overnight stays will be in beautiful alpine properties.

The tour will cover four countries in Central Europe; Germany, Austria, Italy and Switzerland. It starts and ends in the Italian city of Milan and will be led by a MotoRover guide on a motorcycle. We will also have a support van at the back of the group for luggage and assistance during the ride. Apart from the thrilling rides and great hotels, the route also promises beautiful views and interesting sights and activities.

This is the ultimate motorcycle tour of the Alps. DO NOT MISS THIS.
